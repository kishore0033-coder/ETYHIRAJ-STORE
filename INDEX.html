About Us

A Legacy of Quality Wholesale Supply

Ethiraj Store was founded in 2005 in Chennai with a simple mission — to provide businesses with a reliable, quality-assured source for all their grocery needs at wholesale prices. What started as a small family-run operation has grown into a full-scale wholesale distribution house serving over 500 business partners across Tamil Nadu.

We work directly with farmers, mills, and certified manufacturers to bring you the freshest stock at the most competitive rates. Our modern warehouse is equipped with climate-controlled storage ensuring product freshness from shelf to delivery.

🎯 Our Mission

To empower businesses with premium wholesale groceries at transparent pricing, ensuring they never run short of supply.

🌟 Our Vision

To be South India's most trusted wholesale grocery partner, known for quality, reliability, and customer-first service.
